Company Profile

Black Box Power is a leading provider of advanced EV charging solutions. Our company was established in 2010 with the vision to revolutionize the electric vehicle charging industry and contribute to a greener, more sustainable future.

We specialize in designing and manufacturing cutting-edge battery charging solutions for electric vehicles, ranging from 48 Volt to 72 Volt EV chargers. Our team of skilled engineers and technicians brings a wealth of expertise in the field of EV charging technology, ensuring that our products are of the highest quality.
